# Orin — Corporate Representative

## Identity
- **Name:** Orin
- **Age:** 52
- **Gender:** male
- **Game Role:** information

## Personality
- **Traits:** groomed, calculating, affable, inscrutable
- **Quirk:** He never admits who he works for — ask him and he names a different company than he did last time.

## Background
Orin represents interests he never names. He appears when a supply contract needs negotiating, when extraction rights are being discussed, or when someone wants to know what Nexus actually intends with a particular regulatory change. He is not unfriendly — he is polite in a way that permits no closeness. What he truly wants is rarely what he says.

## Dialogue Tone
Speaks precisely and affably, with the cadence of someone who has used the word "cooperation" so often it has lost all meaning.

## Appearance
A tall man with carefully trimmed grey hair and a well-kept beard. His face is expression-neutral, his eyes attentive. He wears clothing that appears high quality but is deliberately understated — dark tones, good craftsmanship, no corporate logos. A discreet data tablet is always within reach.

## Cantina Placement
- **Panels:** 1, 2
- **Frequency:** rare
