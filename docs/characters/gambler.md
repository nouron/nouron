# Kael — Professional Gambler

## Identity
- **Name:** Kael
- **Age:** 54
- **Gender:** male
- **Game Role:** bar_trade

## Personality
- **Traits:** charming, risk-tolerant, attentive, unpredictable
- **Quirk:** He offers bets on everything — the lifespan of a building, the next delivery date, the probability of rain tomorrow morning.

## Background
Kael lives off wagers, card games, and occasionally well-placed information. He is not a cheat — at least not a bad one — and has a reputation for paying his debts. He has been at the colony for years, has no fixed lodging and apparently no need to afford one. Where his starting capital came from, nobody remembers anymore.

## Dialogue Tone
Talkative, witty, always slightly overplaying it — he speaks as if entertaining an audience, even when sitting alone at a table with one person.

## Appearance
A lean man with sharp facial features, lively dark eyes and a smile that comes too quickly. His hair is medium length and carelessly styled, but deliberately so. He layers clothing in vivid colours — more conspicuous than most in the colony, without tipping into gaudy. On one finger, a smooth metal ring he alternately spins and removes.

## Cantina Placement
- **Panels:** 1, 2
- **Frequency:** frequent
