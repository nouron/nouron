# Zara — Professional Gambler

## Identity
- **Name:** Zara
- **Age:** 54
- **Gender:** female
- **Game Role:** bar_trade

## Personality
- **Traits:** charming, risk-tolerant, attentive, unpredictable
- **Quirk:** She offers bets on everything — the lifespan of a building, the next delivery date, the probability of rain tomorrow morning.

## Background
Zara lives off wagers, card games, and occasionally well-placed information. She is not a cheat — at least not a bad one — and has a reputation for paying her debts. She has been at the colony for years, has no fixed lodging and apparently no need to afford one. Where her starting capital came from, nobody remembers anymore.

## Dialogue Tone
Talkative, witty, always slightly overplaying it — she speaks as if entertaining an audience, even when sitting alone at a table with one person.

## Appearance
A lean woman with sharp facial features, lively dark eyes and a smile that comes too quickly. Her hair is medium length and carelessly styled, but deliberately so. She layers clothing in vivid colours — more conspicuous than most in the colony, without tipping into gaudy. On one finger, a smooth metal ring she alternately spins and removes.

## Cantina Placement
- **Panels:** 1, 2
- **Frequency:** frequent
