# Dax — Smuggler

## Identity
- **Name:** Dax
- **Age:** 50
- **Gender:** male
- **Game Role:** bar_trade

## Personality
- **Traits:** quiet, cautious, mistrustful, pragmatic
- **Quirk:** He always sits with his back to the wall and never leaves the cantina through the main entrance.

## Background
Dax has been flying routes for twenty years that appear in no Nexus registry. He knows which depots wave through unregistered cargo and which patrols are distracted at certain times. He has no loyalties — only prices. He comes to the colony whenever something is needed that cannot be obtained through official channels.

## Dialogue Tone
Speaks quietly, almost too quietly, and pauses where other people would keep talking — as if weighing whether the next sentence is safe to say.

## Appearance
A medium-height man with sun-darkened skin and a face that looks a few years older than it is. Short dark brown hair with first hints of grey, a three-day stubble. He wears practical, neutral clothing — a worn work jacket with several inner pockets, plain trousers, sturdy boots. No distinctive badges or markings.

## Cantina Placement
- **Panels:** 2, 3
- **Frequency:** occasional
