# Juno — Retired Mercenary

## Identity
- **Name:** Juno
- **Age:** 61
- **Gender:** female
- **Game Role:** bar_trade

## Personality
- **Traits:** calm, disciplined, clear-eyed, reliable
- **Quirk:** She never speaks about specific jobs — only about principles, costs and consequences; ask her something and you get a question back.

## Background
Juno has worked for various clients — none of them with a name that appears in any public directory. Two years ago she stopped taking active contracts. She does not say why. She is at the colony because she knows no one here, and that was initially the main reason. By now she occasionally offers security consulting, nothing active.

## Dialogue Tone
Speaks deliberately and economically, choosing words with the care of someone who knows they carry consequences; no hesitation, no evasion.

## Appearance
A medium-height woman with an athletic, compact build and a calm, watchful expression that never quite relaxes. Her dark hair is closely cropped. She wears simple civilian clothing with no tactical extras — but the way she sits and reads the room says more than the clothing does.

## Cantina Placement
- **Panels:** 2, 3
- **Frequency:** occasional
