# Sarka — Exhausted Mechanic

## Identity
- **Name:** Sarka
- **Age:** 45
- **Gender:** female
- **Game Role:** bar_trade

## Personality
- **Traits:** exhausted, sarcastic, competent, honest
- **Quirk:** She cannot stop assessing machinery — even in the cantina she checks hinges, tests pressure valves, and mutters comments about poor maintenance.

## Background
Sarka keeps the generator station and three quarters of the colony's building distribution systems running. She sleeps too little, eats irregularly, and has accumulated more overtime hours than anyone will ever pay. She comes to the cantina when she needs a moment, but even then her mind keeps running. Her assessments can be trusted — she sugarcoats nothing.

## Dialogue Tone
Speaks tersely, often cutting herself off mid-sentence — not from rudeness, but because she is already on to the next problem; sarcasm is her default setting.

## Appearance
A medium-height woman with dark hair tied back that never quite comes fully clean of grease. Her face is striking, with dark circles under the eyes and a small scar on the chin. She wears a worn work coverall with sleeves rolled up, forearms marked with small burns and abrasions. At least one tool hangs from her belt at all times.

## Cantina Placement
- **Panels:** 1, 2
- **Frequency:** frequent
