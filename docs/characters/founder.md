# Brann — Former Colony Founder

## Identity
- **Name:** Brann
- **Age:** 68
- **Gender:** male
- **Game Role:** story_hook

## Personality
- **Traits:** proud, nostalgic, stubborn, loyal
- **Quirk:** He refers to every location in the colony by its original name — before the renamings, before Nexus, before all of it.

## Background
Brann was there when the first structure modules were set down. He had different plans for the colony than Nexus did — a settlement that sustains itself, not a supply station for corporate interests. Those plans never came to pass. He stayed because he has nowhere else to belong. He watches the Director with a mixture of hope and scepticism.

## Dialogue Tone
Speaks slowly and deliberately, frequently referencing earlier states of things — "back then" is one of his most common phrases; no pathos, just the weight of someone who knows how it could have been.

## Appearance
A large man, now somewhat stooped, with white, unkempt hair and a deeply furrowed face. His hands are marked by years of manual labour. He wears old, multiply patched settler's clothing that was never quite fashionable. On his belt hangs a battered all-purpose knife he has carried since the colony's first day.

- **Panels:** 1, 2
