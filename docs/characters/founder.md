# Aldra — Former Colony Founder

## Identity
- **Name:** Aldra
- **Age:** 68
- **Gender:** female
- **Game Role:** story_hook

## Personality
- **Traits:** proud, nostalgic, stubborn, loyal
- **Quirk:** She refers to every location in the colony by its original name — before the renamings, before Nexus, before all of it.

## Background
Aldra was there when the first structure modules were set down. She had different plans for the colony than Nexus did — a settlement that sustains itself, not a supply station for corporate interests. Those plans never came to pass. She stayed because she has nowhere else to belong. She watches the Director with a mixture of hope and scepticism.

## Dialogue Tone
Speaks slowly and deliberately, frequently referencing earlier states of things — "back then" is one of her most common phrases; no pathos, just the weight of someone who knows how it could have been.

## Appearance
A large woman, now somewhat stooped, with white, unkempt hair and a deeply furrowed face. Her hands are marked by years of manual labour. She wears old, multiply patched settler's clothing that was never quite fashionable. On her belt hangs a battered all-purpose knife she has carried since the colony's first day.

- **Panels:** 1, 2
