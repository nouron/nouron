# Rael — Veteran Prospector

## Identity
- **Name:** Rael
- **Age:** 58
- **Gender:** male
- **Game Role:** bar_trade

## Personality
- **Traits:** pragmatic, approachable, grounded, experienced
- **Quirk:** He automatically appraises the material value of everything around him — he cannot help it.

## Background
Rael has been moving between colonies and outposts for three decades, always searching for deposits that the official survey teams missed. He sells deposit information, occasionally samples or routes he knows better than the charts do. He comes back regularly because the colony is a good staging point and Tomas pours the best beer in the sector — or so he claims.

## Dialogue Tone
Speaks directly and without detour, with a calm, earthy tone — information is delivered like goods, without salesmanship.

## Appearance
A compact, weathered man with broad shoulders and a face deeply bronzed by sun and dust. His hair is short and dark grey, his beard an untended short stubble. He wears heavy prospecting clothes with dust in every seam, a wide belt with tool mounts, and sturdy, multiply repaired boots.

## Cantina Placement
- **Panels:** 1, 2
- **Frequency:** occasional
