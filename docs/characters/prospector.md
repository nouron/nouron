# Fen — Veteran Prospector

## Identity
- **Name:** Fen
- **Age:** 58
- **Gender:** female
- **Game Role:** bar_trade

## Personality
- **Traits:** pragmatic, approachable, grounded, experienced
- **Quirk:** She automatically appraises the material value of everything around her — she cannot help it.

## Background
Fen has been moving between colonies and outposts for three decades, always searching for deposits that the official survey teams missed. She sells deposit information, occasionally samples or routes she knows better than the charts do. She comes back regularly because the colony is a good staging point and Tomas pours the best beer in the sector — or so she claims.

## Dialogue Tone
Speaks directly and without detour, with a calm, earthy tone — information is delivered like goods, without salesmanship.

## Appearance
A compact, weathered woman with broad shoulders and a face deeply bronzed by sun and dust. Her hair is short and dark grey, worn close to the head. She wears heavy prospecting clothes with dust in every seam, a wide belt with tool mounts, and sturdy, multiply repaired boots.

## Cantina Placement
- **Panels:** 1, 2
- **Frequency:** occasional
