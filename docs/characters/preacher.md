# Sorel — Frontier Preacher

## Identity
- **Name:** Sorel
- **Age:** 59
- **Gender:** female
- **Game Role:** story_hook

## Personality
- **Traits:** persuasive, passionate, idealistic, tenacious
- **Quirk:** She quotes no scriptures — she argues as though explaining something self-evident that others have simply not yet seen.

## Background
Sorel is not a wandering preacher in the classical sense — she has settled in the colony and works to influence decisions: how resources are distributed, how strangers are treated, what the colony owes its settlers. She advocates a worldview, not a religion. Whether she is right is a separate question.

## Dialogue Tone
Speaks with quiet, steady conviction — never loud, but hard to interrupt; every sentence sounds like the logical conclusion of a line of thought.

## Appearance
A tall, gaunt woman with long whitish hair tied back, and a narrow, expressive face with deep-set eyes. She wears plain frontier clothing in muted tones, nothing religious — but always with an old, hand-printed cloth around her neck that she changes as though it carries meaning.

## Cantina Placement
- **Panels:** 1, 2
- **Frequency:** occasional
