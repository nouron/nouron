# Lenn — Unlicensed AI Researcher

## Identity
- **Name:** Lenn
- **Age:** 43
- **Gender:** male
- **Game Role:** information

## Personality
- **Traits:** withdrawn, obsessive, restless, brilliant
- **Quirk:** He holds partial conversations with someone who isn't present — whether it's a program running in his earpiece or something else entirely is unclear.

## Background
Lenn used to work for a Nexus-affiliated research facility, until he pushed too far outside the approved parameters. What exactly he is developing, he won't let on. At the colony he makes use of cheap computing capacity and poor network oversight, because nobody looks too closely. He occasionally needs components that don't appear in the regular catalogue.

## Dialogue Tone
Speaks quickly and erratically, switching subjects mid-sentence but always looping back — conversations with him require patience and a good memory.

## Appearance
A gaunt man with a pale complexion and slightly reddened eyes, as though he has been staring at screens for too long. His hair is unkempt, dark blonde and overdue for a cut. He wears a washed-out pullover and an old data jacket with several self-applied patches. An earpiece with a small LED indicator is almost always active.

- **Panels:** 3
