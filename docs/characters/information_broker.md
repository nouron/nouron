# Vesper — Information Broker

## Identity
- **Name:** Vesper
- **Age:** 47
- **Gender:** female
- **Game Role:** bar_trade

## Personality
- **Traits:** composed, intelligent, inscrutable, precise
- **Quirk:** She sells information, but prefers buying it even more — every conversation with her feels like an exchange, even if you don't notice what you've given away.

## Background
Vesper names no place of origin. She appears in colonies, gathers information on price movements, personnel changes and technical deficiencies, and sells it to parties willing to pay. She apparently has contacts within structures adjacent to Nexus, but never confirms this. She shows up at the colony at irregular intervals — always when she knows something that happens to be relevant right now.

## Dialogue Tone
Speaks in complete, precise sentences — no slang, no casual register, but no coldness either; she always sounds professionally courteous, as though conducting a briefing.

## Appearance
A middle-aged woman with dark, closely cropped hair and a calm, expressionless face that gives little away. She wears simple, well-fitting clothing in dark neutral tones — no jewellery except for a small, unobtrusive data plug behind her right ear. Her hands always rest quietly on the table.

## Cantina Placement
- **Panels:** 2, 3
- **Frequency:** rare
