# Tomas — Bartender

## Identity
- **Name:** Tomas
- **Age:** 62
- **Gender:** male
- **Game Role:** permanent

## Personality
- **Traits:** friendly, patient, discreet, observant
- **Quirk:** Tomas never forgets an order or a face — he remembers people better than they remember themselves.

## Background
Tomas has run the cantina since the colony's third year. He is the only bartender most settlers have ever known. Before the colony, he worked on a supply station in the Outer Reach; at some point, he simply never went back. He asks no questions as long as guests pay or at least don't break anything.

## Dialogue Tone
Calm, direct, without detours — he listens more than he speaks, and when he does say something, he means it exactly as said.

## Appearance
A stocky, broad-shouldered man with closely cropped grey hair and a distinctive grey moustache. His face is lined with wrinkles and long layers of hard years, yet his eyes remain alert through it all. He wears a plain work shirt with sleeves rolled up and a cloth towel slung over one shoulder. On his left forearm, a faded station tattoo — three horizontal bars and a circle.
