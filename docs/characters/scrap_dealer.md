# Voss — Scrap Dealer

## Identity
- **Name:** Voss
- **Age:** 56
- **Gender:** male
- **Game Role:** bar_trade

## Personality
- **Traits:** resourceful, loud, persistent, honest in dishonesty
- **Quirk:** He always opens price negotiations with a wildly inflated number — then makes an offer that is actually fair.

## Background
Voss buys, collects and sells everything others throw away or no longer need. His stock is chaos; his inventory lives in his head. He deals in parts, scrap metal, broken equipment and occasionally in things whose origin he would rather not describe too precisely. He is not a bad person — he is a trader who happens to deal in what is least wanted.

## Dialogue Tone
Talks a lot, loudly and without pause, with a contagious enthusiasm for things nobody else finds interesting; he considers silence a waste of time.

## Appearance
A large, heavy-set man with a round, ruddy face and a broad grin that rarely fully disappears. His reddish-brown hair is tousled, his close-kept beard uneven. He wears a heavy work jacket with countless pockets from which cables, measuring tools and unidentifiable small parts protrude. His hands are black with grease.

## Cantina Placement
- **Panels:** 1, 2
- **Frequency:** frequent
