# Maret — Colony Doctor, Off Duty

## Identity
- **Name:** Maret
- **Age:** 57
- **Gender:** female
- **Game Role:** bar_trade

## Personality
- **Traits:** empathetic, exhausted, incorruptible, caring
- **Quirk:** She listens to everyone who sits across from her — even when she herself wanted no more conversation after a long shift.

## Background
Maret is the only trained physician in the colony. Her working hours officially end at 22:00; in practice, they never do. She comes to the cantina to find some quiet, but settlers know where to find her and come anyway. She has no reserves left for grand gestures, but for each individual patient she finds some.

## Dialogue Tone
Speaks softly and calmly, with the patience of someone who daily soothes angry and frightened people; makes no promises she cannot keep.

## Appearance
A medium-height woman with shoulder-length, greying dark hair that is often half-loose — off duty, but never fully. Her face is warm, but deep exhaustion lines sit around her eyes. She usually still wears her medical work jacket over everyday clothes, the device on her wrist set to silent but not removed.

## Cantina Placement
- **Panels:** 1, 2
- **Frequency:** frequent
