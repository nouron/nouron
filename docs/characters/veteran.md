# Ceth — Military Veteran

## Identity
- **Name:** Ceth
- **Age:** 64
- **Gender:** male
- **Game Role:** information

## Personality
- **Traits:** stern, experienced, direct, disciplined
- **Quirk:** He always sits upright and never drinks more than two glasses — and watches everyone who drinks more than he does.

## Background
Ceth has thirty years of service behind him, most recently as a company commander on outpost security missions along the border of uncharted sectors. He is not at the colony for military reasons — he came looking for someone, never found them, and stayed. He knows security protocols, situation assessments and tactical options, but shares this knowledge only when he considers the occasion worth it.

## Dialogue Tone
Speaks in short, precise sentences with no filler words — like someone who learned that every unnecessary syllable over comms can cost lives.

## Appearance
A tall, lean man with closely cropped white hair and a face that seems composed entirely of straight lines. An old, faded scar runs from his left temple to his jaw. He wears civilian clothing, but carries himself with the posture of someone accustomed to uniform — straight, clear, unadorned.

- **Panels:** 1, 2
