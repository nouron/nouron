# Deva — Military Veteran

## Identity
- **Name:** Deva
- **Age:** 64
- **Gender:** female
- **Game Role:** information

## Personality
- **Traits:** stern, experienced, direct, disciplined
- **Quirk:** She always sits upright and never drinks more than two glasses — and watches everyone who drinks more than she does.

## Background
Deva has thirty years of service behind her, most recently as a company commander on outpost security missions along the border of uncharted sectors. She is not at the colony for military reasons — she came looking for someone, never found them, and stayed. She knows security protocols, situation assessments and tactical options, but shares this knowledge only when she considers the occasion worth it.

## Dialogue Tone
Speaks in short, precise sentences with no filler words — like someone who learned that every unnecessary syllable over comms can cost lives.

## Appearance
A tall, lean woman with closely cropped white hair and a face that seems composed entirely of straight lines. An old, faded scar runs from her left temple to her jaw. She wears civilian clothing, but carries herself with the posture of someone accustomed to uniform — straight, clear, unadorned.

- **Panels:** 1, 2
- **Frequency:** occasional
